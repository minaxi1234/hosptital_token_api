Q: Who can generate patient tokens?
A: Tokens are generated by staff members.

Q: Can patients log into the system?
A: No, patients do not have login accounts.

Q: Where can doctors see their tokens?
A: Doctors can see their tokens in the doctor dashboard.

Q: How are token updates shown in real time?
A: Token updates are shown using WebSocket connections.
Q: What are the OPD timings?
A: OPD services are available from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM.

Q: Is the hospital open on Sundays?
A: OPD is usually closed on Sundays, but emergency services are available.

Q: Do emergency patients need tokens?
A: No, emergency patients are treated immediately.
